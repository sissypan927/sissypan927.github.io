ABOUT LOUIE ZONG

Louie Zong is an artist, author, animator, musician, game developer, and virtuso in all things creative. He's worked on popular children tv shows such as We Bare Bears, The Ghost and Molly McGee, and more. Louie Zong has also been nominated twice for the Emmy Awards for both his writing and animation work on animated shows. Find more of his work on Youtube or on his portfolio!
